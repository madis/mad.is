# Project Ancapia

A plan to create a anarcho-capitalist territory.

## Purchasing the land

A group of libertarian entrepreneurs and investors pool some money together and offer to pay lets say 20% of the current federal government debt (~90 billion USD as of May 2026). In return they get a land for example a 100x100 km area (10000 km²). If hectare costs $100 then this would amount to 100 million USD.

## Rights

The project financiers would be the original owners of this new land. As this land is considered 100% private property, they can choose what to do with it. They'll probably allocate some parts for themselves - for their houses, real estate projects, businesses or factories. Other parts they can either put to sale or think up other ways to put them into good use.

## Trade

At least in the beginning almost everything would have to be imported. This means that unless the transportation of the goods is done through air, the goods need to pass through the land of someone else (other Argentinian provinces or ports). 

## Laws

Ancapia would start with no laws and just the original owner and his land. Probably the owner before selling, renting or giving away parts of the land, will want to put down some rules. These rules are the law. One of the first companies that would be established in Ancapia, as new settlers come, would be a company of security or insurance. It could be an existing company from outside Ancapia or a new one formed inside.
